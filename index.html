About Figueroa Carpentry

Figueroa Carpentry is a family-owned business with over 15 years of experience in the carpentry and remodeling industry. Founded by Juvenal Figueroa, we take pride in delivering exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service to every client.

Our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and dedication to customer satisfaction sets us apart. We work closely with our clients to understand their vision and bring it to life with precision and skill.

  • Licensed and insured carpentry services
  • High-quality materials and workmanship
  • On-time project completion
  • Free estimates and consultations
